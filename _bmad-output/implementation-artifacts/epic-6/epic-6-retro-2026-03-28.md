## What went well

- **Consistent enforcement:** Bidirectional block filtering in feed and matches; messaging returns `BLOCKED_USER` when appropriate.
- **Rate limits:** Reports and blocks reuse the same sliding-window approach as swipes and auth.
- **Account status:** Suspended/banned users get a clear `ACCOUNT_RESTRICTED` path—aligns with moderation outcomes.
- **Discoverability:** Mobile exposes report/block from feed and chat; help is reachable from account.

## Challenges

- **Operator auth:** Shared secret in header is MVP-only—migrate to proper operator identity, rotation, and audit review in production.
- **Cross-feature regressions:** Any new social feature must ask “blocked? reported?”—add to definition of ready for future stories.
- **Help placeholders:** `mailto:` hints are not a full ticket system—track as product debt.

## Insights

- **Safety is a system property:** Retro of Epics 2–3 highlighted match/chat paths; Epic 6 closes the loop with enforcement.
- **OpenAPI 0.6.0** documents safety/operator/help—contract CI protects clients.
