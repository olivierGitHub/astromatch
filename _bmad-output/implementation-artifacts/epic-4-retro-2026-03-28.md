## What went well

- **Alignment with UX-DR4:** Sheet flow and states (submitting, error, retry) support accessibility.
- **Persistence:** Events feed reordering logic server-side while preserving FR14/FR23.
- **Separation of concerns:** Mismatch is not a swipe; separate events avoid conflating analytics.

## Challenges

- **Opaque reordering:** Hard to explain in tests—rely on behavior (“relative order changes”) vs “score delta.”
- **Offline queue:** Flush semantics and user messaging when back online need ongoing UX polish.

## Insights

- Same **rate limiter** pattern as swipes prevents abuse of mismatch submission.
- Mismatch feedback volume will inform **safety and quality** initiatives—pipeline is ready for moderation (Epic 6).
